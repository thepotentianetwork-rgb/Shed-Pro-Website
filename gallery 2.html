What We Build With

Materials, Colors & Foundation

The most common questions — answered upfront so there are no surprises.

LP SmartSide Siding

Every ShedPro LLC build uses LP SmartSide engineered wood siding — the industry's top-rated shed siding. Treated to resist fungal decay, termites, and moisture. It holds paint beautifully and is built for the freeze-thaw cycles of Utah and Idaho. Far superior to standard OSB or T1-11 used elsewhere — it won't warp, crack, or delaminate over time.

Any Color You Want

We paint with Sherwin-Williams exterior paint — which means you're not limited to a preset palette. If Sherwin-Williams makes it, we can paint it.

Browse the full library at sherwin-williams.com, pick your color, and give us the name or SW number. We'll match it exactly.

Foundation Requirements

We need a reasonably flat surface — no more than 6 inches of unevenness. We level the ground during the build. Grass, gravel, and concrete all work.

Our Recommendation: A solid concrete pad is always best for longevity. You are responsible for any ground movement over time, so concrete gives you the most stable, long-lasting foundation.
