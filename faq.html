Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Can't find your answer? Contact us directly — we respond fast.

Our #1 most requested style is the A-Frame / Gable — and it's easy to see why. The classic peaked roofline handles Utah snow loads perfectly, works in any backyard, and has timeless curb appeal. Our #2 most requested is the Barn Style, which gives you dramatically more vertical space and loft potential without increasing your footprint. Both styles are available in any size, color, and configuration. Browse all styles →
Pricing varies based on size, materials, and features. We offer free quotes with no obligation — the best way to get an accurate number is to reach out and tell us what you're looking for. We'll give you a clear, itemized quote fast.
Most builds are completed in 1–3 days depending on size and complexity. We build on-site at your property from start to finish. If you're adding custom doors or windows, lead time for those materials may extend the timeline slightly.
Permit requirements vary by city and county. In most Utah cities, sheds under 200 sq ft don't require a permit. Anything larger typically does. We recommend checking with your local planning department before ordering — we're happy to help you figure it out.
Absolutely. You can choose your roof style, siding color (any Sherwin-Williams color), trim color, roof color, doors, windows, and more. Use our free 3D designer to visualize your build before you commit.
We build every shed with LP SmartSide engineered wood siding — not cheap OSB or T1-11. LP SmartSide is engineered to resist moisture, impact, and the freeze-thaw cycles common across Utah and Idaho. It holds paint beautifully and comes with a 50-year manufacturer warranty. Learn more about our materials.
Any color you want — seriously. We paint all of our sheds with Sherwin-Williams exterior paint, which means you have access to thousands of colors, not just the ones shown on our website. Browse the full library at sherwin-williams.com and bring us your pick.
We recommend a concrete slab for the best long-term results, but we can also build on a gravel pad or treated wood skids. Our floors are framed with pressure treated lumber, 16" on center to prevent caving and water damage. We'll walk you through what makes sense for your site.
Yes — we offer pre-wired electrical as an add-on on any build. We have 4 tiers to choose from — Basic, Standard, Core, and Essential — ranging from a single light and outlet all the way up to soffit lighting, multiple switches, and GFCI outlets. A licensed electrician will need to connect the wiring to your home's panel. See all electrical packages.
Yes — every ShedPro LLC build is backed by our 3-year workmanship warranty. Our LP SmartSide siding carries a 50-year manufacturer warranty, our asphalt shingles carry a 40-year manufacturer warranty, and our Sherwin-Williams paint is rated for long-term exterior performance. See full warranty details.
Yes — we offer financing options for qualifying customers, up to $20,000. Ask us about financing when you get your quote and we'll walk you through the details.
We accept cashier's check as our preferred payment method. We also offer electronic invoicing and payment through Invoice2go for your convenience. Financing is also available for qualifying customers.
Shed kits are pre-manufactured in a factory with cheaper materials and then assembled on-site. Our sheds are built from scratch at your property — framed, sided, roofed, and finished on-site with premium materials. The result is a stronger, longer-lasting, fully custom structure that actually fits your space and needs.
Yes — custom doors and windows are available. Our standard windows are aluminum and vinyl single pane, exterior-rated for Utah's climate. Custom options are available but add time to the build while we wait for materials to arrive. Ask us when you get your quote.
Yes — we build ADUs (Accessory Dwelling Units) and other livable structures in addition to standard sheds. These projects involve additional planning, permitting, and customization. Contact us to discuss your project.
