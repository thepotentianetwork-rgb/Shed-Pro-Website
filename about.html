Our Founder

Meet Cristian

It started in high school — a job painting shed trim. Most people would've moved on. Cristian paid attention.

He learned the craft from the ground up, picking up every skill along the way — framing, finishing, customer relationships, the whole thing. Nearly a decade later, he founded ShedPro LLC and brought that same attention to detail to every build under his own name.

Over 200 custom sheds built across Utah, Idaho, and surrounding states. Every one of them touched by the same standard Cristian set for himself from day one.

He works lean — usually just himself and a trusted helper — because he believes the quality of the work reflects directly on the person doing it. You'll never get a crew that doesn't care.

The Journey

How We Got Here

From painting trim in high school to Utah's #1 custom shed builder — here's the story.

The Beginning

First Job — Painting Shed Trim

Cristian's first exposure to the shed industry came in high school — painting trim on sheds. Most people would've moved on. He paid attention to every detail and fell in love with the craft.

The Learning Years

Mastering the Trade

Over several years Cristian learned every aspect of shed building — framing, roofing, siding, finishing, customer relationships. He didn't just want to build sheds. He wanted to build them right.

~5 Years Ago

ShedPro LLC Is Born

Armed with nearly a decade of experience and a standard most people don't even know to demand, Cristian launched ShedPro LLC. The mission: build the best custom sheds in Utah, on-site, every time.

Today

200+ Builds & Growing

Utah, Idaho, and surrounding states. 200+ custom sheds built. A growing list of customers who trusted us with their backyard — and came back to tell their friends. And we're just getting started.
