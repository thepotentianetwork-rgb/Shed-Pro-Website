Premium Materials

What Goes Into Every Build

We use the same high-quality materials on every shed — no upsells, no cheap substitutions. This is the standard.

LP SmartSide Siding

Every ShedPro LLC build uses LP SmartSide engineered wood siding — the industry's top-rated shed siding. Treated to resist fungal decay, termites, and moisture, it holds paint beautifully and is built for the freeze-thaw cycles of Utah and Idaho winters. It won't rot, warp, or delaminate over time.

Sherwin-Williams Paint

We paint with Sherwin-Williams exterior paint — which means you're not limited to a preset palette. If Sherwin-Williams makes it, we can paint it. Browse the full color library at sherwin-williams.com and bring us your pick.

Quality Framing Lumber

We frame every shed with quality structural lumber — properly spaced, squared, and built to carry the load. Our framing is designed to handle Utah's snow loads, wind, and temperature swings without shifting or settling over time.

Asphalt Shingles

We roof every shed with quality asphalt shingles in black, gray, or brown. Rated for high wind and impact resistance — built for the Utah and Idaho climate. Backed by a 40-year manufacturer warranty, your roof stays watertight season after season.

Doors & Windows

Our standard windows are aluminum and vinyl single pane — exterior-rated and built for Utah's climate. Double pane windows are available as an upgrade for better insulation, just note they cost more and may add lead time to your build while we wait for them to arrive. Standard door options include single doors, double doors, and roll-up garage doors. Custom doors and windows are also available — ask us when you get your quote.

Foundation Options

We recommend a concrete slab or gravel pad for the best long-term results. We can help you understand foundation requirements based on your shed size, location, and local code. Concrete is the most durable option and protects your investment long-term.

Pressure Treated Flooring

Every ShedPro LLC floor is built with pressure treated lumber, framed 16" on center. This prevents caving, flexing, and water damage over time — giving you a solid, long-lasting floor that holds up under heavy equipment, tools, and everyday use. No shortcuts on the foundation of your shed.

Why It Matters

Why We Choose LP SmartSide

Not all shed siding is created equal. Here's why LP SmartSide is the only siding we use.

✓ Moisture Resistant

LP SmartSide is engineered to resist moisture absorption — critical for Utah's freeze-thaw cycles and Idaho's wet winters. It won't swell, warp, or rot like standard OSB or T1-11 siding.

✓ Pest Resistant

Treated with zinc borate, LP SmartSide resists termites and fungal decay without you having to do anything extra. It's protection built into the material itself.

✓ Holds Paint Better

The engineered surface accepts paint evenly and holds it longer than natural wood. Your shed keeps its color and finish looking sharp for years without peeling or fading.

✓ Impact Resistant

LP SmartSide is tested to withstand high winds and impact. It meets strict standards for hail, wind-driven rain, and structural loading — so your shed stays protected in severe weather.
